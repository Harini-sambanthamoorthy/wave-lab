Welcome to the Virtual Wave Laboratory

WaveLab is an interactive learning platform to understand how Microwaves and Infrared waves are used in Computer Networks for wireless communication. You can visualize how signals propagate, how obstacles affect transmission, and how parameters like frequency, power, noise, path loss, and bandwidth influence performance.

This lab covers important CN concepts such as: Line of Sight (LoS), Noise & Interference, Path Loss, Propagation Delay, and Bandwidth vs Frequency.
